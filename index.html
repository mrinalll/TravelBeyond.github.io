popular travel packages of the hills

Uttrakhand Package

Explore Uttarakhand's enchanting beauty in this travel package. Begin in Nainital, a picturesque hill station with a serene lake. Visit the revered Naina Devi Temple and enjoy breathtaking views from Tiffin Top. Explore Jim Corbett National Park, India's oldest tiger reserve, on an exhilarating safari. Finally, head to Mussoorie, the "Queen of the Hills," for stunning landscapes and a mountain retreat. Uttarakhand offers natural wonders, adventure, and tranquility, making it ideal for an unforgettable getaway.

Himachal Pradesh Package

Immerse yourself in the mesmerizing beauty of Himachal Pradesh. Explore the charming hill stations of Shimla and Manali, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush valleys. Visit the scenic Rohtang Pass, indulge in adventure sports, and discover the serene Dalhousie. Experience the spiritual vibes of Dharamshala and the ancient monasteries of Spiti Valley. Himachal Pradesh offers a perfect blend of natural wonders and cultural richness, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Jammu and Kashmir Package

Embark on a journey to the paradise of Jammu and Kashmir. Experience the pristine beauty of Srinagar with its tranquil Dal Lake and Mughal gardens. Explore the enchanting valleys of Gulmarg and Pahalgam, surrounded by snow-capped peaks. Visit the sacred Amarnath Cave and experience the unique floating markets of Dal Lake. Jammu and Kashmir offer a breathtaking landscape, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality, making it a dream destination for every travel lover to explore.

Kerala Package

Indulge in the serene beauty of Kerala, aptly called "God's Own Country." Immerse yourself in the rich wildlife of Periyar National Park and indulge in delicious authentic Kerala cuisine. Kerala offers a perfect blend of scenic landscapes, cultural experiences, and rejuvenation.

Darjeeling

Experience the charm of Darjeeling, nestled in the mighty Himalayas.
